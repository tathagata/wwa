Meet Auditi!

Auditi Debroy is a doctor in making - not the kind who gives you pills, but the kind who runs all day in a white lab coat and injects chemical cocktails on rats. But don't let that white lab coat fool you for a moment. She is also the biggest contributor to the various charitable institutions that adorn Chicago's Magnifient Mile. She provides safe shelter for all instyle clothes in her ever shrinking closet, every single season. The fashion industry lovingly calls her "The Savior"!

However, if home cooks got yelp reviews, she would have been a straight up 6 stars. Next time you clueless in Chi-town, looking for some finger licking goodness ... just dial A for multicuisine culinary bliss.

And there's T!

T, Totha, Tathagat, Taata ... or whatever that your tongue allows you to pronounce - is an avant-grade procrastinator. He has only one goal - maximize the amount of work undone. Before he met Auditi, he used to sit idle at one place for days, months and seasons together staring at the terminal. However, after years of painful domestication, he has shown remarkable improvements in areas like personal hygiene, laundry, dishwashing, forgetting fewer items from the grocery! Aetheists Today calls it a Divine Miracle!

He only cares for few things in life. Seeing Auditi happy definitely tops the list.

Two very different people. But connected with the same chord.
